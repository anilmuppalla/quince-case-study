Endpoint Unavailability

For endpoint unavailability, the platform applies the rolling replacement pattern to runtime healing: bad pod stops receiving traffic, a fresh pod comes up healthy, traffic shifts to it, the bad pod drains. High-priority Search overflows to healthy / premium capacity during the readiness gap. Incident-day cost: $59.72 versus $193.54 for peak-provisioned coverage.
